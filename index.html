Track, Organize & Enhance Your Pokémon Collection!

We're building the ultimate tool to help you manage your Pokémon trading card collection, whether you're a seasoned collector or just starting your journey. This app is designed to simplify card tracking, set completion, and collection management while offering unique features to enhance your experience.

Features We're Working On

  • View All English & Japanese Sets: Access a complete library of Pokémon card sets from both English and Japanese releases.
  • Track Card Variants: Manage different card variants with ease, ensuring you stay on top of rare and alternate versions.
  • Check Card Prices & Values: Stay informed about market trends with real-time card values for graded and ungraded cards.
  • Daily Artist Spotlight: Celebrate the creativity behind the cards by discovering new and familiar Pokémon illustrators.
  • Search by Name or Artist: Quickly find specific cards using our powerful search functionality.
  • Create & Share Custom Lists: Build personalized lists of your favorite cards and share them with friends or fellow collectors.
  • Set Completion Stats: See at a glance how much of each Pokémon set you’ve completed with detailed, easy-to-read statistics.
  • Track Collection Growth: Monitor your collection’s progress over time and celebrate milestones.
  • Export Data: Export your collection details as a CSV for easy record-keeping or sharing.

Why We're Excited

  • User-Friendly Design: We're building an intuitive interface that will make managing even the largest collections a breeze.
  • Dark Mode: Perfect for late-night card sorting or browsing.

Help Us Build It!

We're in the process of creating this app and would love your input! If you have suggestions or feature ideas, reach out to us at admin@tradingcardapps.com. Your feedback is invaluable in shaping a tool that serves the Pokémon community.

Join us on this journey to create something truly special for Pokémon card collectors everywhere!
